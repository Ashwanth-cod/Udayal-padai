Vision

"Udayal Padai Charitable Trust is dedicated to providing affordable self-defense training to women in schools, colleges, and organizations. We are committed to our vision of offering free training to women in orphanages, old age homes, tribal and scheduled caste communities, and those along the Parvati line, across Pan India."

Empowering women and girls through yoga, self-defense, and cyberbullying prevention is our mission. With a holistic approach, Udayal Padai Charitable Trust offers accessible programs and is dedicated to reaching underserved communities with free training, ensuring safety and confidence for all.

"At Udayal Padai Charitable Trust, we believe every woman deserves the right to feel safe. We provide affordable self-defense training and are driven by our vision to offer free programs to vulnerable women across India, including those in orphanages, old age homes, tribal communities, and along the poverty line."
